About Us

The Story Behind Kumar Store

Founded in 2010, Kumar Store has grown from a small corner shop to one of the most trusted names in stationery and sports goods in the region.

We pride ourselves on providing high-quality products that inspire creativity and active lifestyles. Our carefully curated selection caters to students, professionals, and sports enthusiasts alike.

At Kumar Stories, we believe that quality stationery enhances learning and creativity, while quality sports equipment encourages an active and healthy lifestyle.

Our Journey

1964

The Beginning

Kumar Store was founded as a small stationery shop in a local neighborhood in a remote town where none of the business were established.

1980

Expanding Product Range

We expanded our product range to include sports and pooja items catering to the local needs which was lead by Late shree Subash kuamr jain .

2000

Moving to a Larger sku

Due to growing popularity, we moved to a larger sku and started keeping cleaning essentials as the town kunkuri had no other business.

2014

Premium Product Line

Introduced our premium line sports equipment for discerning customers and now is on its way to be the supermarket of the town.

2021

Community Engagement

Started hosting workshops and sports events to engage with the local community.

2025

Digital Transformation

Launched our official website to reach a wider audience and provide better service.
